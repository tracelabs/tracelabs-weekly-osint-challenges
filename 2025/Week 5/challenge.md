# Trace Labs Weekly OSINT Challenge - Week 05

Welcome to Week 05 of the **Trace Labs Weekly OSINT Challenge!**  
This week’s theme: **Webcam Geolocation and Historical Weather Verification**

Your task is to analyze a publicly available image and temperature and determine **where is the location and what the temperature is** using open-source intelligence techniques.

---

## Objective

Determine the following based on a provided webcam still:

Location shown in the image

Temperature at 7:56 PM on 10-21-2025
